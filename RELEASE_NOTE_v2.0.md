# eQMS Release Note – Revision 2.0

**Release Version:** v2.0
**Release Date:** 19 June 2026
**Repository:** QMS
**Commit ID:** 1db2569

## Overview

This release introduces significant enhancements to the eQMS platform, expanding capabilities in Quality Management, Inventory Control, Supplier Management, Routine Operations, and Procurement Traceability. The update focuses on improving process control, operational visibility, and compliance readiness.

## New Features

### Quality Management Module

* Instrument Master Management
* Calibration Planning and History Tracking
* Measurement System Analysis (MSA)
* Gauge R&R (GRR) Study Support
* Quality Dashboard and Monitoring Tools

### Routine Management Module

* Routine Category Management
* Checklist Creation and Assignment
* Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Custom Schedules
* Routine Execution Tracking
* Compliance Monitoring and Status Management

### Inventory & Material Management

* Product Master and Product Categories
* Inventory Dashboard
* Material Issue Management
* Enhanced Goods Receipt and Inventory Tracking

### QA Inspection Management

* QA Inspection Creation and Management
* Inspection Documentation Support
* Inspection Record Traceability

### Supplier Management Enhancements

* Approved Supplier List (ASL)
* Supplier Risk Classification
* Supplier Evaluation Improvements
* Supplier Audit Request Workflow
* Supplier Audit Dashboard
* Supplier Document Management

### Procurement & Traceability

* QR Code Generation for:

  * RFQ
  * Quotation
  * Work Order
  * Delivery Challan
* Purchase Order Receiving and Quantity Tracking

## System Improvements

* Form Builder Enhancements
* Workflow Improvements
* Dashboard Modernization
* PDF Document Layout Improvements
* User Interface Refinements
* Data Management and Performance Optimizations

## Database Changes

* New migrations added for Quality, Routine, Inventory, Procurement, and Supplier Management modules.
* Existing models updated to support new business processes and traceability requirements.
